# David Glass for Bastrop County Commissioner, Precinct 4

## Overview
David Glass has spent his life building in Bastrop County. As a business owner, rancher, and community leader for more than four decades, he understands what it takes to manage growth without losing what makes this place home.

Before entering public service, David built and ran a successful national software company. He brought that same operational rigor — tight budgets, real accountability, measurable outcomes — directly to Bastrop County government.

## Platform and Priorities
1. **Roads & Infrastructure**: Maintaining and upgrading the arteries that connect our communities as Bastrop County grows at record pace. He has completed dozens of road projects across Precinct 4 to keep communities connected and safe.
2. **Fiscal Accountability**: Treating every tax dollar carefully and transparently. Budgets should be tight and tied to measurable outcomes. He has provided consistent budgetary restraint to deliver core services without tax hikes.
3. **Water & Growth**: Balancing development with the infrastructure and water resources required to support the communities we already have.

## Biography & Facts
