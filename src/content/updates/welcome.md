Transparency in county government means more than just having an open door at the office. It means actively bringing information to the residents who pay the taxes and live with the consequences of our decisions.

That is why we are launching this new updates section. Moving forward, my office will use this space to provide direct reports on what we are working on.

### What to Expect

1. **Road Work Schedules**: Advance notice on mill and overlay projects affecting your daily route.
2. **Infrastructure Planning**: Updates on drainage clearances and bridge repairs ahead of storm seasons.
3. **County Business summaries**: Plain-English breakdowns of the budget process and where your tax dollars are going.
4. **Community Events**: Opportunities to engage directly with local leadership and organizations in Elgin and eastern Bastrop County.

My commitment is that this will not be a campaign blog. It will be a factual, useful resource for tracking outcomes in Precinct 4.

Stay connected by dropping your email in the "Get Updates" form below, and we will automatically send you the most critical alerts.
